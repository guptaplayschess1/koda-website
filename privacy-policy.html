Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026 · Koda Property Group LLC

This Privacy Policy describes how Koda Property Group LLC ("Koda Property Group," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and handles information you provide when you visit our website at kodapropertygroup.com or contact us about your property.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

When you fill out a contact form, call us, or text us, we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Property address
  • Any details you share about your situation

Information collected automatically

When you visit our website, we may collect standard technical information including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on those pages
  • Referring URL (the page you visited before ours)
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Evaluate your property — to research comparable sales, condition, and local market data relevant to your property
  • Contact you about your inquiry — by phone call, text message, or email, depending on your preference and consent
  • Present a cash offer — if your property is one we can buy
  • Communicate follow-up information — timelines, closing details, and related transaction matters
  • Improve our website — to understand how visitors use our site and make it more useful

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not share your information with marketing companies, list brokers, or other real estate investors.

3. SMS Communications

By providing your phone number and consenting on our contact form, or by texting us directly, you agree to receive SMS messages from Koda Property Group LLC. These messages may include:

  • A response to your property inquiry
  • Follow-up messages about a cash offer
  • Scheduling information for calls or closings
  • Replies to questions you send us

How to opt out of SMS

You can stop receiving SMS messages from us at any time by replying STOP to any text message we send. You may also reply HELP for assistance. Message and data rates may apply depending on your wireless carrier.

Opting out of SMS does not affect your ability to contact us by phone or email, and does not withdraw any offer we have made.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share limited information with:

  • Title companies and escrow agents — if a transaction moves forward, we share necessary details with the title company handling the closing
  • Service providers — we may use tools like Netlify (our website host) and Google (analytics) that process information on our behalf under their own privacy policies. These providers are not permitted to use your information for their own purposes.
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, court order, or valid legal process

5. Data Retention

We retain your contact information for as long as is necessary to:

  • Respond to your inquiry and evaluate your property
  • Complete any transaction you enter into with us
  • Comply with legal and tax recordkeeping obligations

If you would like us to delete your information, see Section 7 below.

6. Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies for basic functionality and to understand how visitors use the site. We use Google Analytics, which collects anonymized usage data under Google's privacy policy. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Doing so may affect some functionality of the site.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You may contact us to:

  • Request a copy of the information we have about you
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate information
  • Ask us to delete your information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt out of SMS communications (reply STOP to any text, or contact us directly)

8. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information, including using HTTPS encryption on our website and limiting access to personal information to those with a business need. No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
